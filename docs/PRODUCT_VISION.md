# Product Vision

**For** writers and readers who want to engage in meaningful discussions
**Who** need a simple platform to share thoughts and commentary
**The** Commentary Blog
**Is a** blogging platform
**That** enables users to publish posts and engage through comments
**Unlike** complex blogging platforms like Medium or WordPress
**Our product** focuses on simplicity and conversation, with an intuitive interface for both writing and commenting

## Success Criteria

- Users can create and publish posts in under 2 minutes
- Comments appear immediately without moderation
- Clean, distraction-free reading experience
